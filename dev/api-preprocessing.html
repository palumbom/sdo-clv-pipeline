Classify solar regions using continuum, magnetogram, doppler, and AIA data.

Parameters:

  • con (SDOImage) – Continuum, magnetogram, dopplergram, and filtergram images. These are used to derive masks for umbrae, penumbrae, quiet sun, network, plage, and moat flow regions.

  • mag (SDOImage) – Continuum, magnetogram, dopplergram, and filtergram images. These are used to derive masks for umbrae, penumbrae, quiet sun, network, plage, and moat flow regions.

  • dop (SDOImage) – Continuum, magnetogram, dopplergram, and filtergram images. These are used to derive masks for umbrae, penumbrae, quiet sun, network, plage, and moat flow regions.

  • aia (SDOImage) – Continuum, magnetogram, dopplergram, and filtergram images. These are used to derive masks for umbrae, penumbrae, quiet sun, network, plage, and moat flow regions.
