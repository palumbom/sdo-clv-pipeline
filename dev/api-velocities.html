Velocities#

Computing radial velocity results from processed SDO data.

sdo_clv_pipeline.sdo_vels
sdo_clv_pipeline.sdo_vels.compute_disk_results(mjd, flat_mu, flat_int, flat_v_corr, flat_v_rot, flat_ld, flat_iflat, flat_w_quiet, flat_w_active, flat_abs_mag, mu_thresh, k_hat_con)[source]#

Compute disk-integrated velocity and intensity metrics.

Parameters:

  • mjd (float) – Observation time in modified Julian date.

  • flat_mu (array-like) – Flattened arrays of mu, intensity, corrected velocity, and rotation velocity.

  • flat_int (array-like) – Flattened arrays of mu, intensity, corrected velocity, and rotation velocity.

  • flat_v_corr (array-like) – Flattened arrays of mu, intensity, corrected velocity, and rotation velocity.

  • flat_v_rot (array-like) – Flattened arrays of mu, intensity, corrected velocity, and rotation velocity.

  • flat_ld (array-like) – Flattened limb-darkening model and flattened intensity.

  • flat_iflat (array-like) – Flattened limb-darkening model and flattened intensity.

  • flat_w_quiet (array-like) – Boolean weights for quiet and active regions.

  • flat_w_active (array-like) – Boolean weights for quiet and active regions.

  • flat_abs_mag (array-like) – Absolute magnetogram values.

  • mu_thresh (float) – Minimum mu to include in the aggregation.

  • k_hat_con (float) – Continuum scaling factor for the photometric term.

sdo_clv_pipeline.legendre.gen_leg_vec(lmax, theta)[source]#

Generate normalized Legendre basis and derivative vs latitude.

This is used by dopplergram bulk-velocity modeling to build design matrices.

Parameters:

  • lmax (int) – Maximum degree of the Legendre series.

  • theta (astropy.units.Quantity) – Colatitude in degrees.

sdo_clv_pipeline.legendre.gen_leg_x_vec(lmax, x)[source]#

Generate normalized Legendre basis and derivative vs x.

This is used by dopplergram bulk-velocity modeling to build design matrices.

Parameters:

  • lmax (int) – Maximum degree of the Legendre series.

  • x (astropy.units.Quantity) – Coordinate in degrees (converted to radians internally).
